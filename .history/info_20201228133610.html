About

I am a software engineer focused on mobile platforms, such as iOS and Andorid. In my spare time, I am a musician, being the bass my main instrument.

I have a degree in Applied Mathematics and Computation and a master degree in Informatics Engineering.

The first years of my professional career were spent not only in coding (mainly C#) but also dealing with several hardware, since I worked for a company that developed software for the hospitality sector.

In my next professional experience I started working with Java, developing enterprise web applications for the Health Care sector.

In 2007, with some colleagues, I co-founded a company, iUZ Technologies. The developed projects has been mainly for the Health Care sector. More information can be checked here.

In parallel, I started developing some work in a area that I am passionate about: mobile devices. I started leaning Objective-C in 2009 (and Swift in 2014). Since then, I launched Tweeton (a Twitter client) in the App Store in 2011. Meanwhile, I also started working with Android.
