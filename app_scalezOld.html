Scalez

Deprecated

The target market of this app is musicians who may find it useful being able to access information about the most common musical scales.

In the details page for each scale, information about the notes within the scale is presented, with additional information about the harmonic field, chords / tetrad chords, additional chords and other information.

Technical features

  • Slide panel: SWRevealViewController
